Social Distancing Checker

   ONLINE WEB BASED SOLUTION

From the beginning of 2020, a global pandemic spread among the wide population, widely known as Severe acute respiratory syndrome coronavirus 2(SARS-CoV-2), due to Corona virus has outbroke almost half of globe. The pandemic has been spreading across the globe to almost 213 countries. Originated from Wuhan, a city in China, the disease has killed around 313400 and 4733858 corona positive patients which is increasing continuosly till May 2020. This fatal disease has proved to be the most dangerous pandemic across the globe by WHO (World Health Organization). Many researchers and scientists as well as doctors has been continuously putting their best efforts to save the corona infected patients. According to WHO, the disease is spreading due to coming in contact with Corona virus. The chain of infection has increasing mainly due to people who are corona infected. So, if we resist coming in contact with people can save us from corona infection. Therefore maintaining distance among population, known as Social Distancing has proved and becoming as a very effective way of prevention from the SARS-CoV-2. Ignoring physical contact among people will not only prevent you but also your family as this micro virus which cannot be viewed with naked eyes can enter your body through physical contact and ruin your healthy life.

Our project, Social Distancing Checker-an online web based solution will help you detect the social distancing around you. It will capture a picture from your website and will trace your GPS location. The in-built algorithm will detect the person in the given picture and will process further to determine whether they are following social distancing or not. This will help you to not only prevent yourself but also, it will make you send the location to security or police officials to help them take care of the place where the persons involved there are not able to follow social distancing. This can save there as well as a wide people around them and their families too from being infected from Corona Virus. Even you can implement this algorithm and the project in your place such as office, shops, public places which are getting crowded in this global pandemic to find and detect whether your place is being followed by social distancing or not.
